About

Hello! I'm Tom Beller,

a seasoned systems engineer with a comprehensive background in designing, implementing, and maintaining IT infrastructures across various environments. With extensive experience at companies like Heartland Business Systems, Warren Distribution, and Airlite Plastics, I've honed my technical skills and developed a robust foundation in both on-premises and cloud-based solutions.

Throughout my career, I have demonstrated proficiency in a wide range of technologies, both on-premises and in the cloud. My expertise includes virtualization, storage management, network configurations, identity management, infrastructure as code, operating systems administration, scripting, backup, disaster recovery, and security.

In addition to my technical expertise, I hold several certifications such as Microsoft Certified: Azure Administrator Associate and Azure Virtual Desktop Specialty. These certifications reflect my commitment to staying current with industry advancements and continuously improving my skill set.

What truly sets me apart is my ability to blend technical knowledge with essential soft skills. I possess strong logical reasoning, problem-solving capabilities, and a keen sense of responsibility. My ability to learn quickly and adapt to new challenges has enabled me to effectively address complex technical issues and deliver innovative solutions under pressure.

In the past two years, I took a deliberate break from formal employment to pursue personal growth and enjoy enriching life experiences. During this time, I stayed engaged with the latest developments in cloud technologies and industry trends, ensuring that I remain at the forefront of the field.

I am passionate about contributing my blend of technical expertise and soft skills to new and exciting projects. I look forward to leveraging my background to drive operational success and help organizations overcome their IT challenges.

Thank you for visiting my personal website, and I invite you to explore further to learn more about my professional journey and the skills I bring to the table.
